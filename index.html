About The Addon

MSD Addon is a comprehensive military-themed addon for Minecraft Bedrock Edition. It introduces a vast array of realistic content, including advanced weapon mechanics, a diverse lineup of land and air vehicles, custom armor, and a sophisticated gameplay system. The goal is to provide an immersive, tactical experience with high-quality models, sounds, and detailed mechanics.

Key Features:

Advanced Gun Mechanics

Experience realistic gun mechanics, including ADS, recoil, multiple fire modes, and a wide range of attachments.

Custom Vehicles

Drive a variety of custom vehicles, from light utility trucks to main battle tanks and attack helicopters.

Leveling System

A complete leveling system to rank up and unlock new gear through gameplay.

Custom UI

Modern and clean UI for a better user experience and seamless interaction with addon features.

Guide

Basic Weapon Controls

Aiming Down Sights (ADS)

How: Sneak/Crouch while holding a weapon

Effect: Activates aiming mode for increased accuracy

Notes: Most weapons have iron sights or optics for precision shooting

Firing

How: Use/Interact button while holding a weapon

Effect: Fires the weapon according to its fire mode

Notes: Mind your ammo count and fire rate

Reloading

How: Automatic when magazine is empty

Effect: Replenishes magazine from reserve ammo

Notes: Reload times vary by weapon class (see weapon stats)

Fire Modes

Semi-Auto: One shot per trigger pull (DMRs, pistols)

Full-Auto: Continuous fire while holding trigger (ARs, LMGs)

Burst: Fixed bursts per trigger pull (some assault rifles)

Weapon Attachments & Optics

Optic Sights

Available: Red Dot, Holographic, 2x, 4x, 6x, 8x scopes

Usage: Equipped variants have built-in optics

Effect: Improved target acquisition at various ranges

Foregrips

Available: Vertical grips on compatible weapons

Effect: Reduced recoil and improved accuracy

Note: Indicated by "_grip" in weapon class name

Suppressors

Available: On designated stealth variants

Effect: Reduced firing sound

Examples: AS VAL (integrated)

Extended Magazines

Available: Drum mags for LMGs and some ARs

Effect: Increased ammunition capacity

Examples: RPK drum (75 rds), PKM belt (200 rds)

Weapon Categories Explained

Assault Rifles

General-purpose rifles with balanced stats

  • Rate of Fire: 600-900 RPM
  • Effective Range: Medium
  • Best For: Versatile combat situations

Battle Rifles

High-damage rifles firing full-power cartridges

  • Rate of Fire: 400-600 RPM
  • Effective Range: Medium-Long
  • Best For: Power and penetration

Carbines

Compact rifles for close-quarters combat

  • Rate of Fire: 600-750 RPM
  • Effective Range: Short-Medium
  • Best For: Mobility and maneuverability

DMRs (Designated Marksman Rifles)

Semi-automatic precision rifles

  • Rate of Fire: 250-300 RPM
  • Effective Range: Long
  • Best For: Accurate mid-range fire

Sniper Rifles

Bolt-action rifles for extreme range

  • Rate of Fire: 40-60 RPM
  • Effective Range: Very Long
  • Best For: Long-range elimination

LMGs (Light Machine Guns)

High-capacity suppressive fire weapons

  • Rate of Fire: 600-800 RPM
  • Magazine: 75-200 rounds
  • Best For: Sustained fire support

SMGs (Submachine Guns)

Compact automatic weapons

  • Rate of Fire: 700-900 RPM
  • Effective Range: Short
  • Best For: Close combat and mobility

Shotguns

Close-range powerhouses

  • Rate of Fire: 57-300 RPM
  • Effective Range: Very Short
  • Best For: Devastating close combat

Heavy Weapons & Equipment

Mortar

Usage: A portable, indirect-fire weapon for high-angle attacks.

1. Deploy: Place the mortar item on the ground.

2. Aim: Interact with the mortar. Use your view to aim; the shell's trajectory is fixed, so range is determined by your distance to the target.

3. Fire: Use the fire button to launch a shell.

M777 Howitzer

Usage: A powerful, long-range artillery piece for devastating bombardments.

1. Deploy: Place the M777 item on the ground.

2. Operate: Interact with the howitzer to open its control UI.

3. Aim: Use the sliders in the UI to precisely adjust the Yaw (horizontal) and Elevation (vertical) angles. The UI will show the estimated range in blocks.

4. Fire: Press the fire button in the UI to shoot.

Gortensiya (Combat Drone)

Deploy: Place the Gortensiya drone item from your inventory. It will hover in place.

Load Ammunition: The drone can be equipped with various munitions:

  • RPG Rockets: Anti-vehicle/structure rockets
  • RGD-5 Grenades: Anti-personnel explosives
  • Mortar Shells: High-angle bombardment rounds

Control: Interact with a Computer item while the drone is deployed to take control. Your view will switch to the drone's camera.

Movement: Use standard movement controls to fly the drone. Use jump/sneak for altitude control.

Fire: Use the fire button to launch loaded munitions at targets.

Exit: Drop or switch from the Computer item to exit drone view.

Note: The drone has limited ammunition capacity - reload by interacting with it while holding ammo items.

M1A2 Abrams

Main Battle Tank

  • Top Speed: 67 km/h
  • Usage: Driver controls movement. Gunner controls main cannon and coaxial MG. Commander controls roof-mounted HMG.

BMP-3

Infantry Fighting Vehicle

Infantry Fighting Vehicle
